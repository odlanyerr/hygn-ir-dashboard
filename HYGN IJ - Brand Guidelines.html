Brand Tone & Design Principles

Eco-Conscious

Green identity signals environmental responsibility. Messaging should reinforce sustainability, eco-friendly products, and clean living. Avoid industrial/harsh tones.

Clean & Sanitary

Blue accent represents sanitation and purity. Layouts should feel uncluttered, hygienic, and professional — reflecting the core service promise.

Pioneer & Trusted

First hygiene system company in Indonesia (2006). 15+ years of track record. Tone should project expertise, credibility, and market leadership.

Modern & Accessible

2023 rebranding unified ecoCare Group with a fresh, modern identity. Communication should be approachable, clear, and accessible to B2B and B2C audiences.
